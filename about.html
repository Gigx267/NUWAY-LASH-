How to Use Nuway Lashes

  1. Carefully remove a lash from the tray using tweezers.
  2. Apply a thin line of adhesive along the lash band.
  3. Let the adhesive set for a few seconds until slightly tacky.
  4. Place the lash on your eyelid, as close to your natural lash line as possible, starting from the outer corner and working your way inwards.
  5. Gently press the lash band down to secure it.
